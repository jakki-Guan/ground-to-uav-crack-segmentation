Supervision-scaling comparison on the fixed UAV hold-out split. Cross markers show the source-only baselines, filled circles at zero labeled UAV samples show the selected B1 reference, solid lines show B2 few-shot performance as target labels increase from `fs05` to `fs20`, and dashed lines denote the full-target reference. B1 is shown as the first target-aware mitigation without target-positive supervision in the training loss and serves as a comparison reference for the B2 trajectory; it is not the initialization point for B2. B2 instead fine-tunes from the frozen source checkpoint and gradually approaches the full-target reference as limited UAV labels are added.
